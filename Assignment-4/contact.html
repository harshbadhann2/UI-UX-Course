❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can participate in the fest?

A: All college students from any institution are welcome to participate. Just bring a valid college ID.

Q: Is there any registration fee?

A: No, participation is completely free! We believe in making our fest accessible to everyone.

Q: Can I participate in multiple events?

A: Yes! You can register for as many events as you want, as long as they don't overlap in timing.

Q: When is the registration deadline?

A: Registration closes on September 10, 2025. Don't miss out - register early!

Q: Are team events allowed?

A: Yes! Most events welcome both individual and team participation. Check event details for specifics.

Q: Will food be available at the venue?

A: Yes! We'll have food stalls and refreshment counters available throughout the fest days.
