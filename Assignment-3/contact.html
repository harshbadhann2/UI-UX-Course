â“ Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the fest?

All college and university students with valid student ID can participate. Some events may have specific eligibility criteria.

Is there any registration fee?

No, participation is completely free for all events. However, accommodation and food are separate if you're from another city.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can register for multiple events as long as there are no time conflicts. Check the schedule page for timing details.

How do I register for events?

Visit our registration page, fill out the form with your details, and select the events you want to participate in.

What should I bring on event day?

Bring a valid college ID, comfortable clothes, your laptop (for technical events), and enthusiasm to participate!

Are there accommodation facilities?

Yes, we provide accommodation for outstation participants. Contact us in advance to book your stay.

What about food arrangements?

Food stalls and cafeteria will be available throughout the fest. We also have options for different dietary requirements.

How are winners selected?

Each event has qualified judges who will evaluate participants based on predefined criteria. All decisions are final.
